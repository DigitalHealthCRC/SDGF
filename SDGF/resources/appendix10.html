Appendix 10 - Safety Assessment (Five Safes)

Assess the adequacy of controls before releasing synthetic data.

Assess the adequacy of controls across the Five Safes model before releasing synthetic data.

Check controls across the Five Safes to confirm synthetic data can be released to the approved audience under agreed conditions.

Tip: Capture links to policies, agreements, or monitoring dashboards that demonstrate each control is in place.

D. Safe Settings

Describe the environment where data will be accessed (secure network, VDI, etc.).*
Summarise authentication, monitoring, and incident response measures.

E. Safe Outputs

Describe how outputs will be checked prior to release/publication.*
