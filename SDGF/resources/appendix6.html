C. Data Fitness and Minimisation

Describe how the source data meet the needs of the proposed use case.*
List minimum data elements necessary for synthesis.
Confirm direct identifiers have been removed or irreversibly treated.*
Identify any sensitive variables retained and rationale for inclusion.
